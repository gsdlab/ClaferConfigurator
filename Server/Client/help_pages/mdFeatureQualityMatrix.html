Feature and Quality Matrix

In this window, you can filter, sort, remove or compare the optimal variants from your model
  • Press to add a require(once) or eliminate(twice) filter.
  • Press to remove a variant from the view.
  • Press to fade out any features that are uniform across the table. Press to restore the initial view.
  • Press to clear any filters you may have active.
  • By default, the view displays all attributes, including the quality attributes for each feature. To hide an attribute from the matrix, add mark it up with a compiler directive //# QUALITY_ATTRIBUTE, for example
    abstract Feature
        cost : integer //# QUALITY_ATTRIBUTE
